# Stage I - Business Objectives

## Business Objectives Identified

- Develop a secure and user-friendly mobile marketplace for sneaker buyers and sellers.
- Protect customer privacy and sensitive financial information during account management and payment processing.
- Provide secure communication features between buyers and sellers while maintaining trust and platform reliability.

The application must support smooth transactions, secure user authentication, multiple payment methods, and responsible handling of customer information to avoid legal and reputational risks.
